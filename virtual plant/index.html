🌱 Plant Growth Information

Stages of Plant Growth

  1. Seed: The plant starts as a seed.
  2. Germination: The seed sprouts roots and shoots.
  3. Seedling: Young plant starts growing leaves.
  4. Mature Plant: Fully grown plant ready to produce flowers/fruits.
  5. Reproduction: Plant produces seeds to start a new cycle.

Tips for Healthy Plant Growth

  • Provide enough sunlight.
  • Water regularly, but avoid overwatering.
  • Use good quality soil.
  • Protect plants from pests.
  • Fertilize occasionally for nutrients.

Fun Fact 🌿

Some plants can grow hundreds of feet tall, like the giant redwoods! Others are small and delicate, but every plant follows the same basic growth stages.
